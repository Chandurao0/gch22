

## Financial Account Tracker

A local-first app to track your bank accounts, transactions, and budgets — all data stored in your browser with no login required.

### Pages & Layout

- **Dashboard** — Overview of all bank accounts with total net worth, individual account balances, and recent transactions
- **Accounts** — Add/edit/delete bank accounts (name, bank, type: checking/savings/credit, currency)
- **Transactions** — Full list of transactions with filtering by account, category, and date range; add/edit/delete transactions (amount, date, description, category, account)
- **Budget** — Set monthly budgets per category, see progress bars showing spent vs. budget

### Key Features

1. **Multi-account management** — Add multiple bank accounts with balances that auto-update based on transactions
2. **Transaction logging** — Record income and expenses with categories (food, transport, bills, etc.), assign to specific accounts
3. **Category budgets** — Set monthly spending limits per category with visual progress indicators
4. **Summary cards** — Total balance, monthly income, monthly expenses, and net savings displayed on the dashboard
5. **Data persistence** — All data saved to browser localStorage so it persists between sessions

### Design
- Clean, modern dashboard with card-based layout
- Color-coded categories and account types
- Responsive design for mobile and desktop
